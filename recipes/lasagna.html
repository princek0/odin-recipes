Lasagna

Description

Brief Step-By-Step

Here's a brief overview of what you should expect when you make lasagna:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
    6. Let lasagna rest before serving.

Ingredients

Here are all the ingredients you should expect to use:

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg
